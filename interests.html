My Interest

They are many things I love such as listening to music, learning about fitness, eating healthy food, reading books and the most thing I love is doing research about philosophy.

The person inside the picture is the famous sufisme philosophy which I really idolize; from his private life to his contribution to poetry world. He is really inspire me and changed my perspective of many things in this world.

His name is Jalāl ad-Dīn Muhammad Rūmī, also known as Jalāl ad-Dīn Muhammad Balkhī or Mevlânâ/Mawlānā which means "Our Master". Most people call him as Rumi

Most poet written by him is about love, life and kindness which I feel very beautifully written by him.

Example of the poem is "Being a candle is not easy; in order to give light, one must first burn."
